1. Realme Narzo N20A

The Realme Narzo N20A is one of the most balanced budget smartphones available in 2025, offering a solid mix of performance, design, and battery life for under ₹10,000. Powered by the MediaTek Helio G85 processor paired with 4GB RAM, this phone is optimized for daily multitasking like web browsing, video streaming, and light gaming. Its 6.5-inch HD+ display with 90Hz refresh rate delivers smooth visuals, making scrolling and watching videos enjoyable. The Realme UI based on Android 14 is clean and fast, giving a near-stock experience with some added customization options.

One of its biggest highlights is the 5000mAh battery, capable of lasting a full day even with heavy usage. The dual rear camera setup, led by a 13MP main shooter, captures sharp images in daylight, while software optimizations improve low-light shots. With 64GB storage and expandable memory, you won’t run out of space for apps, media, or documents. This makes it a reliable companion for students, working professionals, and casual users looking for an affordable yet capable smartphone.

  • Processor: MediaTek Helio G85
  • Display: 6.5-inch HD+ with 90Hz refresh rate
  • RAM & Storage: 4GB + 64GB (expandable)
  • Battery: 5000mAh with fast charging
  • Cameras: 13MP + AI lens dual rear camera
  • OS: Realme UI based on Android 14
