1. boAt Airdopes Alpha

The boAt Airdopes Alpha stands out as one of the most popular TWS earbuds in the under ₹1500 category. It offers a premium design with a matte finish, giving it a classy look while still being compact enough to fit in your pocket. The earbuds are equipped with 10mm dynamic drivers that produce deep bass, clear vocals, and balanced treble. Whether you are into Bollywood, EDM, or podcasts, the sound signature remains pleasant and non-fatiguing. Gamers will appreciate the low-latency Beast Mode, which reduces audio delay during gaming sessions, making footstep sounds in FPS games more accurate.

Battery life is another area where the Airdopes Alpha shines. The earbuds alone last around 6 hours per charge, and the case extends that to nearly 24 hours. The ASAP fast-charging feature is a lifesaver, providing up to 90 minutes of playback with just 5 minutes of charge — perfect for last-minute commutes. The IPX4 rating ensures sweat and splash resistance, making them ideal for workouts. Touch controls allow you to play/pause music, change tracks, and activate voice assistants with simple taps. Overall, the boAt Airdopes Alpha is an excellent choice for budget-conscious users seeking style, performance, and durability in one package.

  • 10mm Dynamic Drivers with Deep Bass
  • Beast Mode for Low-Latency Gaming
  • Battery Life: 24 hours (with case)
  • ASAP Charge: 5 min = 90 min Playback
  • Bluetooth 5.1, IPX4 Water Resistance
  • Touch Controls + Voice Assistant Support

2. Noise Buds Uno

The Noise Buds Uno are perfect for those who prioritize call clarity and long battery life. They come with quad-mic Environmental Noise Cancellation (ENC), which significantly cuts down background noise during calls, making them excellent for professionals and students attending online meetings. The sound profile is clean and slightly bass-heavy, providing a fun listening experience. The Bluetooth 5.2 connectivity ensures stable pairing and reduced lag, which is great for streaming videos or gaming.

Battery life is a huge win here, with a total of up to 45 hours (9 hours per earbud charge + case recharges). The earbuds also support fast charging, giving you 180 minutes of playtime in just 10 minutes of charge. The Noise app allows you to customize touch controls, adjust EQ settings, and even locate your earbuds if you misplace them. The IPX4 water resistance ensures that sweat and light rain won’t be a problem. For anyone who needs great call quality, massive battery life, and an affordable price, the Noise Buds Uno are a no-brainer.

  • Quad-Mic ENC for Crystal-Clear Calls
  • 45 Hours Total Battery Backup
  • Fast Charging: 10 min = 3 hrs Playback
  • Bluetooth 5.2 with Stable Connectivity
  • IPX4 Splash & Sweat Resistant
  • Noise App Support with EQ Customization

3. Realme Buds T110

Realme Buds T110 are known for their crisp sound and super reliable connectivity. With 10mm drivers, they produce a bass-forward sound profile perfect for music lovers. The 40ms Super Low Latency Gaming Mode is a boon for gamers, making them one of the best TWS options for under ₹1500 if you play BGMI or COD Mobile. The earbuds also feature AI ENC for clearer calls and come with an ergonomic design that fits comfortably for long hours.

Battery life is excellent with 6 hours per charge and up to 28 hours with the case. The case is compact, and the earbuds magnetically snap in place. IPX5 water resistance is slightly better than competitors, making them more durable. Touch controls are accurate, and Realme Link App support allows OTA updates, EQ customization, and gesture remapping.

  • 10mm Bass-Boosted Drivers
  • Super Low Latency (40ms) for Gaming
  • Battery: 28 hours total
  • AI ENC for Calls
  • IPX5 Water Resistance
  • Realme Link App Support

4. boAt Airdopes 131 Pro

The Airdopes 131 Pro is a true budget king. Its 13mm drivers deliver surprisingly punchy sound, with good mids and highs. It comes with ENx technology for clear call quality. With a feather-light design of just 3.5g per earbud, you can wear them for hours without fatigue. The 45 hours battery backup with Insta Charge is amazing for binge watchers. It also supports IWP technology for instant wake-and-pair every time you open the case.

Gamers will enjoy the low latency mode, and the IPX4 resistance ensures basic protection against splashes. The case has an LED battery indicator, which is rare in this price range. Overall, for under ₹1300, this is one of the best all-rounders.

  • 13mm Massive Drivers
  • Battery Life: 45 hours total
  • ENx Tech for Calls
  • Insta Wake N Pair Support
  • Low Latency Mode for Gaming
  • Feather-Light 3.5g Earbuds

5. Mivi DuoPods C1

Mivi DuoPods C1 are made in India and boast powerful 13mm drivers tuned for deep bass. The sound profile is bold and loud, making them ideal for bass lovers. The earbuds have a snug fit and passive noise isolation that blocks most outside noise. Each earbud can be used in mono or stereo mode, which is handy if you take calls frequently.

The battery life is impressive — 8.5 hours per charge and 50+ hours with the case. The earbuds also come with fast charging support. Bluetooth 5.3 ensures a rock-solid connection and better efficiency. The dual mic setup handles calls decently well, and touch controls are responsive.

  • Made in India
  • 13mm Deep Bass Drivers
  • Battery: 50+ hours total
  • Bluetooth 5.3
  • Fast Charging Support
  • Mono/Stereo Flexibility

6. Boult Audio Z40

Boult Z40 is a premium-looking TWS with quad-mic ENC and ultra-low latency for gaming. The 10mm boomX bass drivers deliver thunderous bass. It supports Zen Mode ENC which works surprisingly well in noisy surroundings. Battery life is 60 hours — the highest on this list — and it even supports USB-C fast charging.

The IPX5 rating means it is resistant to sweat and splashes. The earbuds also feature touch controls for music, calls, and voice assistant activation. If you want battery beast earbuds under ₹1500, Z40 is unbeatable.

  • Quad Mic ENC
  • Battery: 60 hours total
  • USB-C Fast Charging
  • Ultra-Low Latency Gaming Mode
  • IPX5 Splashproof
  • boomX Bass Drivers

7. pTron Bassbuds Duo

pTron Bassbuds Duo are the most affordable pair in this list yet pack decent features. They offer passive noise cancellation, Bluetooth 5.1, and 32 hours of total playtime. The sound quality is tuned for heavy bass, which casual listeners will love.

The design is compact, and each earbud weighs just 3g. The case is lightweight and pocket-friendly. Touch controls are present, but slightly sensitive. They also feature IPX4 protection, making them sweatproof.

  • Affordable Price
  • 32 Hours Battery Backup
  • Bluetooth 5.1
  • Touch Controls
  • IPX4 Splash Resistant
  • Passive Noise Isolation

8. Truke BTG Storm

The Truke BTG Storm is designed specifically for gamers with its 40ms ultra-low latency. It also has RGB lighting, giving it a gaming aesthetic. The 13mm drivers deliver good sound quality, and the ENC mics ensure clear voice chats during games.

Battery backup is solid at 50 hours. The case supports fast charging, and the earbuds fit snugly in the ears. IPX5 rating ensures sweat and splash resistance. If gaming is your priority, Truke BTG Storm is a great option.

  • Gaming RGB Design
  • 40ms Ultra-Low Latency
  • Battery: 50 hours
  • Fast Charging
  • ENC Mics for Gaming Calls
  • IPX5 Rated

9. Zebronics Sound Bomb 9

Zebronics Sound Bomb 9 is a solid contender offering 10mm drivers with clear vocals and punchy bass. The case has a unique transparent design. The earbuds feature quad mic ENC for better call quality. Latency mode makes it usable for casual gaming.

It offers 38 hours of battery backup, IPX4 protection, and Type-C fast charging. For users looking for stylish design plus decent sound, these are a great choice.

  • Transparent Case Design
  • Quad Mic ENC
  • 38 Hours Battery
  • Type-C Fast Charging
  • Gaming Mode
  • IPX4 Splash Resistant

10. Wings Phantom 315

The Wings Phantom 315 are another gaming-centric earbuds with a bold design and LED indicators. They offer 40ms low latency and dedicated Game Mode. The sound quality is bass-heavy and tuned for immersive gameplay and music sessions. ENC mics do a decent job during voice chat.

The total battery backup is around 50 hours, and the case supports fast charging. IPX5 rating makes them sweatproof. The touch controls are very responsive, and the earbuds have a comfortable in-ear fit.
