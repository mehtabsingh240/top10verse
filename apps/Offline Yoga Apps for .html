Practice yoga anytime, anywhere with these beginner-friendly apps — all work offline!

1. Yoga for Beginners - Daily Yoga

Type: Beginner Yoga | Size: ~45 MB

This app is one of the most downloaded beginner yoga apps for a reason. It is designed for people who are new to yoga and need a gentle start without feeling overwhelmed. The app features short and simple yoga sessions that are explained step-by-step, so you can easily follow along. You can choose between 10-minute warm-ups, 20-minute full body sessions, and 30-day yoga challenges that build your habit slowly. Each session can be downloaded for offline use, making it perfect for practicing at home, in a park, or even when traveling without internet access.

What makes it stand out is its focus on **progress tracking and habit building**. You can set reminders, earn streaks, and even log how you feel after each session. This helps you stay consistent and motivated. The app also includes a few meditation tracks to improve your breathing and mental focus. Overall, this is a must-download if you are just getting started and want an easy, structured way to add yoga to your daily routine.

2. Down Dog

Type: Customizable Yoga | Size: ~60 MB

Down Dog is one of the most flexible yoga apps because it generates a new routine every time you practice. You can select your yoga style (Hatha, Vinyasa, Yin, Restorative), your level (Beginner 1 to Advanced 3), and how long you want to practice (5 to 90 minutes). The app then automatically creates a sequence for you. It also allows offline downloads, so you can practice in airplane mode or when traveling.

The best part is its **voice guidance and background music customization**. You can pick calming instrumental music or nature sounds while practicing, making each session feel unique and personal. Beginners can slow down the pace to clearly understand each asana, while intermediate users can turn up the intensity for a sweat session. The variety is endless, so you’ll never get bored — perfect for long-term motivation.

Download

3. Yoga Studio by Gaiam

Type: HD Video Classes | Size: ~80 MB

Yoga Studio is like carrying a yoga teacher in your pocket. It comes with more than 70 HD video classes professionally recorded and sorted by level, goal, and duration. Whether you want to focus on relaxation, strength, or flexibility, you can find a class tailored to your needs. It also allows you to build your own custom yoga class by picking individual poses and arranging them in your preferred order.

Offline mode is fully supported, and you can sync your classes with your calendar, making it easier to stay consistent. The app’s video quality is excellent, and instructions are easy to follow. If you prefer a polished, studio-like experience with a touch of professionalism, this is one of the most premium yoga apps available for both Android and iOS.

Download

4. Simply Yoga

Type: Simple Guided Yoga | Size: ~35 MB

As the name suggests, Simply Yoga keeps things simple. It has guided video sessions ranging from 20 to 60 minutes. Each session is beginner-friendly and easy to follow, focusing on basic stretches and poses. The app doesn’t bombard you with complex features or premium plans. It just helps you get on the mat and start moving — which is exactly what beginners need.

The offline mode ensures you can access all your sessions even without the internet. If you are looking for a no-nonsense, straightforward yoga app that just works, Simply Yoga is a reliable pick. Perfect for those who want to keep things minimalistic.

Download

5. Glo

Type: Premium Yoga & Pilates | Size: ~90 MB

Glo is a premium yoga app featuring thousands of classes taught by world-class instructors. You can explore multiple yoga styles, Pilates, and even guided meditations. Beginners can start with structured introductory programs, and as you progress, you can explore advanced flows and workshops. All classes can be downloaded for offline viewing.

One of the standout features is Glo’s personalized recommendations based on your activity. It helps you build a schedule that suits your lifestyle and goals. If you’re serious about yoga and willing to invest in your wellness journey, Glo is worth every penny.

Download

6. Asana Rebel

Type: Fitness-Focused Yoga | Size: ~75 MB

Asana Rebel combines yoga with fitness training, making it ideal for users who want to lose weight or tone their bodies while improving flexibility. It offers yoga-inspired workouts like power yoga, fat-burning flows, and core strength routines. The app includes nutrition tips, healthy habit reminders, and even mental wellness exercises to make your transformation holistic.

All sessions can be downloaded for offline use, and progress tracking keeps you motivated. If you want yoga with a modern fitness twist, Asana Rebel is the right choice.

Download

7. Daily Yoga

Type: All-in-One Yoga App | Size: ~85 MB

Daily Yoga is one of the most popular yoga apps globally. It features 500+ asanas, 70 yoga programs, and a worldwide yoga community to keep you engaged. You can pick beginner sessions, advanced challenges, or even weight loss-specific routines. Guided voice instructions and video demos make it beginner-friendly.

Its offline mode lets you download your favorite sessions, so you never miss a practice. The app also includes breathing exercises and meditation guides. If you want an all-in-one yoga solution, this is the app to go for.

Download

8. Pocket Yoga

Type: Self-Paced Yoga | Size: ~50 MB

Pocket Yoga allows you to practice yoga anywhere, anytime. It has over 300 pose illustrations with detailed explanations. You can choose between different practice durations and difficulty levels. The app tracks your progress, calories burned, and total practice time.

Everything is available offline, so you truly have a yoga teacher in your pocket. Its simplicity and clear design make it perfect for solo learners who want to build a consistent home practice.

Download

9. Daily Burn

Type: Yoga + Fitness Classes | Size: ~100 MB

Daily Burn isn’t just a yoga app — it’s a full fitness platform. It includes yoga, HIIT, strength training, and pilates classes. You can join live group sessions or pick on-demand workouts. For yoga lovers, it offers beginner to advanced classes with expert instructors.

You can download videos for offline use, making it great for travelers. If you want to combine yoga with overall fitness training, Daily Burn is an excellent choice.

Download

10. Fitify Yoga

Type: Smart Yoga Trainer | Size: ~60 MB

Fitify is known for its AI-based workout planning. Its yoga section creates personalized sessions based on your goals — strength, flexibility, or relaxation. You can select duration, intensity, and equipment (yoga mat only or with props) to get a fully customized session.

All workouts are available offline, and the app adjusts difficulty based on your feedback. If you want smart yoga coaching that grows with you, Fitify Yoga is a great option for 2025.
