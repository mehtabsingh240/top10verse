Yoga for Beginners - Daily Yoga

Type: Beginner Yoga | Size: ~45 MB

Yoga for Beginners by Leap Fitness is a beginner-friendly yoga app with guided sessions. It’s designed for those starting their yoga journey. Step-by-step video instructions, beginner-friendly routines, a 30-day challenge, and no equipment required make it ideal. Offline access means you can practice anywhere. Its simplicity is its strength, great for total beginners. Practice at home without a studio or equipment. If you're new to yoga, this is one of the best offline apps to start with!

Down Dog

Type: Custom Yoga | Size: ~60 MB

Down Dog is one of the most popular yoga apps globally. It provides personalized yoga classes with high-quality video and audio. Thousands of sequences, adjustable difficulty and pace, and offline downloads make it versatile. You can choose from multiple yoga styles like Vinyasa and Hatha. Choose your level, style, and focus, and Down Dog generates a new flow every time. You’ll never get bored. Perfect for beginners seeking variety and personalization.

Yoga Studio

Type: HD Classes | Size: ~80 MB

Yoga Studio by Gaiam offers professional video classes and is like having a yoga teacher at home. It includes 70+ ready-made classes with HD video, offline access, and a calendar to schedule your sessions. You can select a class or build your own. It’s polished, well-guided, and suitable for all levels. Ideal for users who want structured, studio-like yoga practice right at home.

Simply Yoga

Type: Basic Yoga | Size: ~35 MB

Simply Yoga is an easy-to-use app for beginners, guiding users through basic yoga routines. It features 20, 40, and 60-minute sessions with voice and video demonstrations. It works completely offline, with a simple layout and no unnecessary distractions. Ideal for absolute beginners who want a straightforward yoga app for daily use.

Glo

Type: Premium Yoga | Size: ~90 MB

Glo offers yoga, Pilates, and meditation classes taught by world-class instructors. It includes 4,000+ classes across styles like Vinyasa, Hatha, Yin, and Restorative. You can download videos offline and follow beginner-friendly programs. Ideal for serious learners who want premium content and the ability to track their progress.

Asana Rebel

Type: Fitness Yoga | Size: ~100 MB

Asana Rebel combines yoga-inspired fitness with mindfulness. It includes hybrid workouts, personalized plans, wellness tips, and offline support. With beautiful design and flexible features, it's perfect for beginners who want both yoga and fitness in one app. Great for toning and healthy living.

Daily Yoga

Type: Social Yoga | Size: ~85 MB

Daily Yoga is a community-driven app with guided classes, challenges, and 500+ asanas. It offers offline HD video classes for weight loss, flexibility, and relaxation. You can join challenges and track your progress. Ideal for those who enjoy community and variety in their practice.

Pocket Yoga

Type: Fully Offline | Size: ~60 MB

Pocket Yoga allows you to practice yoga anywhere without internet. It features 27 sessions of varying length and difficulty, voice and visual instructions, and a full pose dictionary. One of the best yoga apps for travelers or offline practice anywhere, anytime.

Yoga Workouts by Daily Burn

Type: Mobility Yoga | Size: ~75 MB

This app offers beginner-friendly yoga and mobility classes with expert instructors and community forums. Programs are tailored to help beginners gradually build up their yoga routine. Videos can be downloaded offline, making it great for new learners on a schedule.

Fitify: Yoga

Type: Custom Workouts | Size: ~50 MB

Fitify’s Yoga app offers customizable yoga and stretching routines. It includes 75+ exercises, a voice coach, progress tracking, and complete offline access. Choose goals like flexibility or strength, and follow routines easily. It’s modern, flexible, and very beginner-friendly.
