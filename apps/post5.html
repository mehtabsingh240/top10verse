Top 10 Math Solving Apps with Camera Scan

1. Photomath

Photomath uses your camera (or manual input) to instantly recognize and solve math problems—ranging from simple arithmetic to calculus. With detailed, step-by-step walkthroughs, it emphasizes understanding "how" and "why" to reach the answer. The free version covers most basics, while Premium adds extra features like visual explanations.

  • OCR-powered photo scanning
  • Step-by-step solutions and explanations
  • Works with handwritten and textbook problems
  • Free with optional Premium upgrade

2. Mathway

Mathway lets you camera-scan or type problems in algebra, trigonometry, calculus, etc., delivering instant answers. Free users get solutions; premium unlocks detailed steps. Its broad coverage makes it the math equivalent of a Swiss Army knife.

  • Camera scan + manual entry
  • Covers a wide range of math topics
  • High accuracy; intuitive interface
  • Free answers; premium for step-by-step

3. Microsoft Math Solver

Microsoft Math Solver is a robust, free tool that recognizes camera scans, typed input, and handwriting. It handles algebra, calculus, graphing, and units—all with detailed guided steps and neat visuals. Ideal for students wanting a fully free, no-ads experience.

  • OCR and handwriting recognition
  • Graphing and unit-solving tools
  • Step explanations with visuals
  • Completely free and ad‑free

4. Socratic by Google

Socratic by Google scans questions in math (and other subjects) and delivers AI-curated step-by-step solutions. It also includes curated articles and video content (Khan Academy, etc.) to reinforce concepts—great for contextual learning beyond quick answers.

  • Multi-subject camera scanning
  • Step-by-step math explanations
  • Curated learning resources
  • Free and easy to use

5. Cymath

Cymath offers OCR and manual math solving with clear step-by-step solutions. It covers algebra, calculus, and more. The simple, fast interface makes it great for quick math help, and premium removes ads and speeds up access.

  • Camera + text input
  • Expands through algebra and calculus
  • Clear step breakdowns
  • Free with optional premium version

6. MathMaster

MathMaster tackles camera-scanned math problems—including word problems—and offers 24/7 access to tutoring. It spans curriculum from pre-algebra to calculus, offering step-by-step examples and optional live tutor support.

  • Word problem scanning and solving
  • Covers all levels up to calculus
  • Access to live tutors
  • Free base level; subscription for more

7. QANDA

QANDA uses camera OCR and AI to solve math problems while offering AI-generated flashcards, micro-lectures, and community Q&A. It’s ideal for students who want an active learning community and personalized revision tools.

  • OCR-powered scanning
  • Stepwise answers and community Q&A
  • AI flashcards and micro lectures
  • Free with premium content

8. AI Math Scanner

AI Math Scanner is a straight-to-the-point app that lets you scan math problems and get instant solutions. Covering basic arithmetic to algebra, it’s lightweight and fast—great for quick homework checks.

  • Simple camera-based solver
  • Instant answer display
  • Covers arithmetic and algebra
  • Free and minimal UI

9. Yup

Yup connects you with live tutors via chat. Simply snap a pic of your math problem and chat with a Tutor who walks you through the solution. It supports curriculum from basic math up to AP Calculus and is excellent for personalized guidance.

  • Submit photo via chat
  • Live tutor assistance
  • Covers up to AP-level math
  • Paid per session or subscription

10. Brainly (with scan)

Brainly’s homework app allows students to scan questions—including math problems—and receive peer-generated answers and explanations. Covering multiple subjects, it's community-driven and free, though answer accuracy can vary.

  • Scan homework via camera
  • Peer-explained answers
  • Covers math, science, and more
  • Free with optional premium for no ads
